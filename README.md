## Business Validation Coach
Test your business idea in 5 minutes with this interactive validation tool. Pressure test your own business idea or start with a simulated petsitting business expansion.
🔗 [Try it live →](https://claude.ai/public/artifacts/3d8310ed-b258-4585-9591-ac7aa371ae5e) 

**What it does**

Business Validation Coach is an AI-powered coaching tool that walks you through a structured 5-minute conversation to validate a new business idea before you commit to it.

You can either:

**Follow a guided scenario** — step into the shoes of a petsitting business owner choosing between three expansion options (pet insurance, grooming partnership, or group daycare), 
or
**Bring your own idea** — describe any business concept and get a tailored validation conversation.

**No data from your conversation is shared with the artifact creator.**

**Requirements**

1. You'll need a Claude.ai account (free or paid)
2. You need to authenticate in Claude.ai. NOTE: If the URL does not load after authentication and you receive a 'Page not found' error on Claude.ai, simply reload and it will work.    

**The coach guides you through six steps:**

Why that idea? — Articulate your reasoning out loud
Build the idea — Get clear on your customer and the problem you're solving
Assumptions check — Separate what you know from what you're guessing
The killer assumption — Identify the one hypothesis that, if wrong, kills the whole thing
Design a real test — Land on a behavioral test you can run in 24 hours (not a survey)
Validation snapshot — Walk away with a one-page summary and a concrete action plan

**How to use the live artifact**

Open the live link in your browser: https://claude.ai/public/artifacts/3d8310ed-b258-4585-9591-ac7aa371ae5e
Choose "Let's go →" to start the petsitting scenario, or "I have my own business idea →" to validate something of your own
Work through the conversation — answer honestly, push back if something doesn't fit
At the end, view your full breakdown: validation snapshot, 5-step action plan, and recommended tools

**How to copy and remix this artifact**

Open the live artifact
Click "Remix" in the top right corner — this opens the artifact in your own Claude workspace
From there you can edit the code, swap out the system prompt, change the scenario, or adapt the hypotheses for a different industry

You'll need a Claude.ai account (free or paid) to remix.
